Hole pattern

Holes are distributed every 20mm in each direction. No more random, useless holes on your robot.

Materials

Plates can be 3D printed, CNC machined or laser cut. You can choose the material you want to use.

Design and reusability

The hole pattern makes it easy to design holders and modules for your robot. You can also reuse those that you already have.

Support ORP on Kickstarter!

Kickstarter campaign for the first ORP chassis has just been launched! It is a part of make 100 project so there are only 100 chassis available for the backers. Each chassis is a kit of parts (steel plates, 3D printed parts, screws and motors) needed to assemble your very own 160mm ORP chassis. There is a discount for the early birds so hurry up and get yours now!

By contributing and backing the project you are not only going to get a chassis but also support the development of the project. The funds raised will be used to develop the next version of the chassis and to create a community around the project.

The progress can be tracked on the widget below, it ends on the first of March 2024. If you want to read more about it click the button below to go to the Kickstarter page. Thank you very much for your support and contribution to the standardized and easy to use future of robotics!
